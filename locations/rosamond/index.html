Rosamond IEE FAQs

Do you serve Southern Kern Unified School District?

Yes. We work appropriate LEAs in Kern County, including Southern Kern Unified, to provide Independent Educational Evaluations.

Do you travel to Rosamond?

Yes. Our evaluators travel to school sites and homes in Rosamond and Willow Springs for in-person testing and observations.

How quickly can assessments be scheduled?

We generally schedule the initial assessment session within 1-2 weeks of receiving the signed contract from the school district.
