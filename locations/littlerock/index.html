Littlerock IEE FAQs

Do you work with the Keppel Union School District?

Yes. We conduct Independent Educational Evaluations for students attending schools within the Keppel Union School District in Littlerock and Pearblossom.

Are your evaluators bilingual?

Yes. We have bilingual (English/Spanish) School Psychologists and Speech-Language Pathologists available to serve the diverse Littlerock community.

What if I live in Pearblossom or Sun Village?

Our service area includes all surrounding communities, including Pearblossom, Sun Village, and Juniper Hills.
