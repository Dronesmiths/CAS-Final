Lancaster IEE FAQs

Do you serve Lancaster School District?

Yes. We provide Independent Educational Evaluations for students attending Lancaster School District, Eastside Union School District, Westside Union School District, and Antelope Valley Union High School District.

Where does the evaluation take place?

Evaluations can be conducted in-person at the student's school, in the home, or at a neutral location in Lancaster. We also offer secure telehealth options.

How quickly can you start an evaluation in Lancaster?

Once we receive the signed Assessment Plan (AP) and contract from the district, we can typically schedule the initial observation or testing session within 1-2 weeks.
