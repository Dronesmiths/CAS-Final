Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about Independent Educational Evaluations and the CAS assessment process.

What areas do you serve?

We provide Independent Educational Evaluations throughout all of California, with both in-person and telehealth options available.

Who can request an IEE?

Parents have the right to request an Independent Educational Evaluation at public expense if they disagree with their school district's assessment. Districts can also contract directly with us for evaluations.

How long does an evaluation take?

We complete comprehensive evaluations and deliver detailed reports within 30 days of signed consent. This includes testing, observations, and report writing.

Are your evaluators licensed?

Yes. All CAS evaluators hold appropriate state licenses and credentials, including Licensed Educational Psychologists (LEP), Speech-Language Pathologists (SLP), Occupational Therapists (OT), Physical Therapists (PT), and Adapted PE specialists.
