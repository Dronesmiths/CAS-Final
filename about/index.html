The Founder's Story

Rooted in Educational Advocacy

Comprehensive Assessment Services was founded by Donald Ghrist, LEP, with a clear mission: to provide California families and school districts with truly independent, high-quality educational evaluations. After witnessing too many students receive inadequate or biased assessments, Donald established CAS to set a higher standard.

We've grown into a trusted resource for Independent Educational Evaluations across California. Our multidisciplinary team includes Licensed Educational Psychologists, Speech-Language Pathologists, Occupational Therapists, Physical Therapists, and Adapted PE specialists‚Äîall committed to accurate, compassionate assessments.

Our Evaluation Process

Professional IEE service from start to finish.

1

Referral

Parents request an IEE or districts contract directly. We handle all consent forms and records requests.

2

Assessment

Our specialists conduct comprehensive testing, observations, and interviews across all relevant domains.

3

Report

Within 30 days, we deliver a detailed written report with findings and specific IEP recommendations.

4

IEP Support

Our evaluators attend IEP meetings to present findings and collaborate with the team.
